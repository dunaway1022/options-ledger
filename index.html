Options Ledger

Your options trades, synced live to a Google Sheet you own. Nothing is stored anywhere except your Google account.

Step 1 · Connect this app to Google

Paste the OAuth Client ID from your Google Cloud project (see SETUP.md for the 5-minute walkthrough).

Step 2 · Sign in

Sign in with the Google account that will hold your trading sheet.

Step 3 · Connect your sheet

Create a brand-new tracker sheet, or paste the ID of an existing one.
